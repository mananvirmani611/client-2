Welcome to Swiss NeWater India

Joint Venture with Forbes Marshall Pvt. Ltd.

Swiss NeWater Holding in Switzerland has concluded a joint-venture agreement with Forbes Marshall and the name of the JVC is “Swiss NeWater India Private Limited”.





The JVC is manufacturing the products to be supplied / distributed in India.

Since its foundation in 1926, Forbes Marshall, one of the leading private companies in India (ranked as a top 10 preferred employer in Asia), stands for innovation, reliability and sustainability. Its R&D efforts have resulted in several award-winning products.

Main activities of Forbes Marshall are steam solutions for industry (boilers, clean & safe factory), instrumentation solutions for industry (process efficiency / energy saving), digital solutions for industry (overall plant automation and remote diagnostics).
